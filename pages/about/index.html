About Us

Dedicated to providing the best possible internet connectivity solutions for marine and coastal communities, backed by our state-of-the-art technology, customized services, and experienced team of professionals.

Our Story

Welcome to HappyHourHeroes, a leading provider of internet connectivity solutions for marine, marina, anchorage, and coastal communities. We’re passionate about connecting people and communities with high-speed and reliable internet access, no matter where they are. Our mission is to make internet connectivity accessible and convenient, and to help our customers stay connected and productive in the most remote and challenging environments.

Founded in 2020, HappyHourHeroes has been providing public wifi and WISP solutions to hundreds of customers. We understand the unique challenges of providing internet connectivity in marine and coastal environments, and we’ve developed customized solutions to meet the specific needs of our customers.

Reliability & Performance

Our public wifi access points are available at marinas, ports, anchorage locations, and coastal businesses, providing seamless connectivity for boaters and coastal residents. We use state-of-the-art technology and a multi-ISP backhaul infrastructure to ensure that our customers always have a fast and reliable internet connection, even in remote locations.

In addition to our public wifi solutions, we also offer WISP services that are tailored to meet the needs of boats and coastal businesses. Our WISP solutions utilize the same multi-ISP backhaul infrastructure as our public wifi, and are designed to provide fast and reliable internet access on the water or on land.

More Than Just A Provider

At HappyHourHeroes, we’re more than just a provider of internet connectivity solutions - we’re a partner that works closely with our customers to understand their specific needs and provide customized solutions that work for them. We’re committed to providing the best possible services and support, and we’re constantly innovating and investing in the latest technology to make sure our customers have the best possible experience.
