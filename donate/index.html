Serve Kindness, Support Dreams

Donate to Empower Service Industry Families

At HappyHourHeroes, we believe in the power of community and the spirit of hospitality to transform lives. As a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization, we’re dedicated to providing essential childcare assistance to the hardworking single parents of the service industry. But we can’t do it alone. Your donations are vital in helping us continue our mission and expand our impact. Here’s how we aim to raise funds, with every dollar going directly towards supporting those who serve our communities:

Paired Dinners

Imagine a world where your love for exquisite cuisine supports a greater cause. Our Paired Dinners are curated experiences that blend the art of cooking with the heart of giving. Collaborating with renowned chefs and sommeliers, we host exclusive dining events where proceeds contribute to our childcare programs.

Your donation here isn’t just a meal; it’s a lifeline for a single parent striving to provide for their family.

Bar Takeovers

Cheers to change! Our Bar Takeovers bring together mixologists and bars nationwide for guest shifts that do more than serve drinks; they serve hope.

By participating or contributing, you’re raising awareness and funds, ensuring single parents in the hospitality industry have access to reliable childcare, allowing them to work with peace of mind.

Merchandising for a Cause

Wear your support on your sleeve! Our merchandise isn’t just branded; it’s a badge of honor, a sign of your commitment to making a difference.

From apparel to accessories, all profits from our merchandise go straight to HappyHourHeroes, funding childcare assistance programs that empower single parents to thrive in their careers and their lives.
