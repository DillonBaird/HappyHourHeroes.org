Book a Sitter - Coming Soon!

Safe, Reliable, and Compassionate Childcare at Your Fingertips

Exciting News for Our Service Industry Families

We at HappyHourHeroes are dedicated to empowering the remarkable single parents in the hospitality industry with reliable, flexible, and affordable childcare solutions. With anticipation building, we’re eager to unveil our soon-to-launch service: a straightforward, accessible way to book vetted, compassionate sitters directly through our website. And for those who already have a trusted sitter but face financial hurdles, we’re introducing a reimbursement program to support your childcare choices.

What’s On the Horizon:

  • Effortless Online Booking: Soon, you’ll enjoy the ease of securing childcare with just a few clicks, tailored to fit your unique schedule and needs, whether for regular shifts or unexpected occasions.

  • Your Trusted Sitter, Our Support: Recognizing the invaluable peace of mind that comes from using a sitter you trust, we’ll offer a reimbursement program. This means you can continue with the childcare arrangements that work best for you and your family, supported by HappyHourHeroes.

  • Safety and Trust at the Forefront: Whether choosing a sitter from our vetted network or opting for your personal trusted caregiver, rest assured that safety and reliability remain our uncompromising priorities.

  • Accessible Care for Our Heroes: Driven by our mission, we strive to make quality childcare affordable. Through the generosity of our community and donors, we aim to provide these services at little to no cost to you.
