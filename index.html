Empowering Service Industry Families, One Shift at a Time

Welcome to HappyHourHeroes, a beacon of hope and support for the unsung heroes of the service industry. Founded in 2023, our mission is simple yet profound: to provide comprehensive childcare assistance to incredible single parents navigating the demanding world of hospitality.

A Helping Hand When You Need It Most

At HappyHourHeroes, we understand the unique challenges faced by single parents in the service industry. That’s why we’ve created a tailored solution to one of the biggest hurdles: childcare. Our comprehensive program offers:

  • No & Low-Cost Childcare: Access to quality childcare should never be a luxury. We provide no and low-cost options for qualified single parents.

  • Verified Babysitters Network: Trust and safety are paramount. Our network of babysitters undergoes a rigorous verification process to ensure your peace of mind.

  • Community Support: Beyond childcare, we offer a supportive community, sharing resources, advice, and experiences to uplift and empower one another.

Our Story

It all began with a hashtag - #SupportYourLocalBarMom. This powerful movement sparked in 2023 aimed to shed light on the struggles faced by single mom bartenders striving to balance the scales of work and family. Inspired by their resilience, HappyHourHeroes, Inc. was born. Today, we extend our reach to all single parents in the service industry, offering a lifeline through our network of verified babysitters, funded entirely by our nonprofit’s efforts. We stand as allies, champions for those who make our dining experiences memorable, yet face unseen challenges every day.

By the Numbers

  • 10.9 million single parents in the US, with 80% being mothers.

  • Service industry marriages, particularly among bartenders and servers, end at twice the rate of other professions.

  • An estimated 3.5 million parents work tirelessly in the restaurant industry, of which 1 million are single moms.

  • Alarmingly, 40% of these courageous mothers live in poverty.

These statistics are more than numbers; they are a call to action. At HappyHourHeroes, we are committed to making a difference, one family at a time.
